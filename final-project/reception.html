Indoor Venue

The venue for your wedding reception is the most important decision you will make. The venue should be a place where you and your guests will have an amazing time, and it should reflect your personality and style.

Venues for weddings can be categorized into two types: indoor venues or outdoor venues. If you are looking for an indoor venue, then there are many to choose from, such as a banquet hall, hotel ballroom, or even a restaurant.

Food Services and Setup

There are many options for food service and setups. Some people prefer to have a buffet style of service while others would like to have a more formal set up.

The buffet style is typically less expensive because there is no need to hire waiters/waitresses or bus staff. The downside of this option is that it can be difficult to serve the guests and the food may not be as fresh as it would be if freshly cooked and served.

The formal set up usually consists of a waiter/waitress serving each course with some food items being prepared in the kitchen ahead of time so they are ready when needed. The downside of this option is that it can be expensive because there are many more costs involved in terms of hiring staff, purchasing materials, etc.
